## 📖 Overview

AI Rockstars is the most comprehensive database of AI Agent influencers and thought leaders across the globe. This project maps the global landscape of AI Agent content creators, providing detailed profiles, collaboration strategies, and market insights for businesses looking to leverage influencer marketing in the AI space.

### 🎯 Key Features

- **Global Coverage**: 6 major regions (North America, East Asia, Europe, South America, South Asia, Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia)
- **Multi-Platform Analysis**: YouTube, X.com (Twitter), LinkedIn
- **Detailed Profiles**: Professional backgrounds, follower counts, collaboration potential
- **Market Insights**: Regional strategies, cultural considerations, ROI analysis
- **Actionable Intelligence**: Ready-to-use collaboration frameworks and contact strategies
